The MINEX token forms the foundation of our ecosystem, enabling fractional ownership of critical minerals and precious metals, governance participation, and revenue distribution through our innovative staking mechanism.

Staking Mechanism

01

Token Acquisition

Investors acquire MINEX tokens through private sale, pre-sale, or public offering, with each token backed by a diversified portfolio of critical minerals and precious metals.

02

Mine Pool Selection

Token holders stake their MINEX into specific mine pools (American Tungsten, Winston Gold, etc.), allocating their investment to their preferred mineral assets.

03

Capital Deployment

Staked capital is deployed to fund mine expansions, new production legs, or operational improvements, creating a direct link between investment and physical asset development.

04

Revenue Distribution

As mines generate revenue, a percentage flows back to the staking pool and is distributed proportionally to token holders based on their stake, creating a sustainable yield mechanism.
